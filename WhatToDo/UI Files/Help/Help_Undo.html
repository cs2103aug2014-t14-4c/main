Back                        Help - Undo your Action!

 Undo your Action:
  • Enter "/undo" to undo your last action. Do this as many times as you wish.

Undo your Action through a Button:
  • Click on the green backward sign above this display panel to undo your last action. Do this as many times as you wish.

Undo your Action using Hotkey:
  • Press Ctrl-Shift-s to undo your last action. Do this as many times as you wish.
